Our Journey

The Feed with Fefe Foundation embarked on a mission to fight hunger in Lagos. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a community-focused force against child hunger.

Compassion

Our foundation is built on compassion. We believe every child deserves the right to adequate nutrition and a healthy start in life.

Dedication

Through dedication and tireless effort, we aim to make a tangible difference in the lives of many children and families in Lagos.

Community

Our strength lies in community involvement. We collaborate with local partners to ensure our programs are effective and impactful.

Mission & Vision / Values

Explore our various initiatives aimed at combating hunger and improving lives in Lagos. Your support can make a real difference.

Vision

Feed with Fefe is committed to nurturing a world where every child's hunger is met with compassion and care. We envision a society where every individual, regardless of their background or circumstances, can rely on us to provide nourishment and support. Our vision is to eradicate hunger by ensuring food security for all, promoting inclusivity, and upholding the inherent dignity of every person we serve. We strive to create a global community where hunger is not only addressed but eliminated, fostering a future where every child has the opportunity to thrive.

Mission

The mission of Feed with Fefe is to provide nutritious meals and food security to vulnerable children in government schools and individuals living on the streets. We aim to alleviate hunger, improve health outcomes, and promote socio-economic development in our communities. Our goal is to ensure that every child and person in need has access to regular and reliable meals, fostering a society where no one goes hungry and everyone can thrive with dignity. We are dedicated to bringing hope, compassion, and sustainable change to those who need it most.

Values

Integrity: We are committed to maintaining a strong moral compass and conducting ourselves with honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our endeavors.

Compassion: We show deep empathy, care, and concern for the well-being of the individuals and communities we serve.

Respect: We treat every individual and community with utmost dignity, appreciating and embracing diversity, while cultivating inclusive practices that create a sense of belonging for all.
